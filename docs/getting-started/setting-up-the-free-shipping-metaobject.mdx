# Free Shipping Metaobject

This theme uses Metaobject to handle the Free shipping bar threshold in order to keep consistent amounts accross multiple currencies. In this tutorial we're going to show you how to setup the Metaobject the Free shipping bar uses.

:::tip Check your spelling
Before you begin note that you must name the new Metaobject `Free shipping` and the money field must be named `Threshold`.
:::

## Step 1 - Add a new Metaobject

Navigate to Content -> Metaobjects in your theme admin.

Then click "Add defininition".

![Adding a new metaobject defininition](./img/free-shipping-bar-1.png)

## Step 2 - Name your new Metaobject

Name your new Metaobject `Free shipping`

![Naming your new metaobject](./img/free-shipping-bar-2.png)

## Step 3 - Add a field to the Metaobject

Add a new field to your Metaobject of type 'Money'.

:::tip
Save time: use the search bar to find the money field.
:::

![Add a money field to your new metaobject](./img/free-shipping-bar-3.png)

## Step 4 - Name the new field

Name the new field `Threshold`. It's nice to add a description of 'Amount', but not necassary.

![Name the money field to your new metaobject](./img/free-shipping-bar-4.png)


## Step 5 - Save your new Metaobject

Hit save. You're almost done.

![Save your new metaobject](./img/free-shipping-bar-5.png)

## Step 6 - Add a new entry

Once your new Metaobject has been saved, you can add a new entry to it. Click on new entry.

![Save your new metaobject](./img/free-shipping-bar-6.png)

## Step 7 - Set your threshold

Set your threshold. This number will be set in your primary currency and update for other currencies if you sell in multiple markets.

![Save your new metaobject](./img/free-shipping-bar-7.png)

## Wrapping update

That's it! You made it through. You can now enable the Free shipping bar and it will function as intended.

![Working Free Shipping Bar](./img/free-shipping-working.gif)
