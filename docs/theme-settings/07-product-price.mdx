# Product price

Control how product pricing is displayed across your store. Keep styling consistent between Product pages, product cards, and the cart.

## Sale price color

Choose how discounted prices appear when a product is on sale. This setting helps visually distinguish sale pricing and draw attention to promotions, while staying consistent with your store’s design.

* **Default** – Uses your theme’s standard text color.
* **Subtle Opacity** – Applies a slightly transparent style for a softer appearance.
* **Accent** – Highlights the sale price using your theme’s accent color.
* **Custom** – Lets you manually select a custom color for full control over the styling.
